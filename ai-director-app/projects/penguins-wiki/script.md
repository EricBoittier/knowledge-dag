# Penguins Wiki Script

## seg_001
Penguin: Penguins are a group of flightless semi-aquatic sea birds which live almost exclusively in the Southern Hemisphere.

## seg_002
Etymology: Only one species, the Galapagos penguin, lives at, and slightly north of, the equator.

## seg_003
Systematics and evolution: Highly adapted for life in the ocean water, penguins have countershaded dark and white plumage and flippers for swimming.

## seg_004
Anatomy and physiology: Most penguins feed on krill, fish, squid and other forms of sea life which they catch with their bills and swallow whole while swimming.
